About Me

Mathematics educator, instructional leader, and ed-tech innovator with over 7 years transforming how students learn maths across Lagos and Nigeria.

Mathematics Educator · Lagos, Nigeria
My Story

A Mathematician Who Teaches With Purpose

Building mathematical minds, one lesson at a time.

I’m Opeoluwa Jamiu Olasunkanmi, a Lagos-based Mathematics educator with over seven years of experience teaching the Nigerian curriculum across JSS and SS levels. I believe every student has the capacity to love mathematics — they just need the right guide.

My career spans classroom teaching, instructional leadership, teacher coaching, and ed-tech product development. I have served as an Education Officer at Lagos State TESCOM, a Teaching Fellow at Teach For Nigeria, and Vice Principal at Roche International School.

Beyond the classroom, I’m building Calculia — an interactive mathematics platform designed specifically for West African students preparing for WAEC, NECO, JAMB, IGCSE, and SAT examinations.

My Teaching Philosophy

📐 Curriculum Mastery
Deep alignment with NERDC and Lagos State Scheme of Work. Lesson plans that make even complex topics accessible and engaging.
🎮 Gamified Learning
Gamified lesson experiences that have measurably improved student attendance and engagement in my classrooms.
💻 Ed-Tech Innovation
Developing Calculia — a digital platform bringing interactive maths to every student regardless of location.
🏆 Measurable Outcomes
Students consistently achieve strong results in WAEC, NECO, BECE and JAMB with documented performance improvements.
